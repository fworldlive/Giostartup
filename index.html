Company Overview

Gio Startup is a dynamic business consulting firm dedicated to empowering entrepreneurs, startups, and growing businesses to achieve sustainable success. With expertise in IT projects, business consulting, startup funding, and business networking, we provide end-to-end solutions that transform innovative ideas into thriving enterprises.

Our mission is to be the one-stop growth partner for startups and businesses, offering strategic guidance, technology solutions, funding support, and networking opportunities that help businesses scale with confidence.
